About us:

Our name has its source in the novel "On the way" by the American writer Jack Kerouac from 1957, based on the author's and his friends' travels around the United States. This book was included, inter alia, at 55. on the list of the 100 best English-language novels of the twentieth century.

The best-selling novel describes the journey of Sal Paradise, Dean Moriarty and Marylou through the USA and is based on a real the author's expedition. The character of Marylou reflects the real-world Luanna Henderson, 15 years old became the wife of one of the heroes of "On the way".
